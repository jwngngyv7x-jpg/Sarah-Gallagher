How to Brew a Medicinal Tea

  1. Use 1-2 teaspoons of dried organic herbs.
  2. Pour 8oz of near-boiling water over the herbs.
  3. Cover the cup to keep the medicinal oils inside.
  4. Steep for 10 minutes, strain, and enjoy.
